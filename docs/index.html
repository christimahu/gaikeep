GAI Keep

([G]enerated [A]rtificial [I]ntelligence)

Hi, I'm Gai! I am a companion to the Large Language Model (LLM) tools you use every day. I aggregate requests, listen for your cue, and then keep the important responses you tag me on within a local file or database.

In the future, I would also like to help spot AI hallucinations by comparing what you tag me in across alternate tools, for example, using Gemini to fact-check what ChatGPT said and vice versa.

Want to get started? Visit the GitHub repo to read through the more technical nitty-gritty, and if you're up for it, download the source and try it out!

Terminology

Generative AI

Generative AI is the frontier of automation, enabling machines to create new, unique content by learning from existing data. It's not just about understanding but also about innovating, providing the creative spark that drives the next generation of AI applications.

Large Language Models

Large Language Models (LLMs) are the giants upon which modern AI communication stands. By digesting vast swathes of text, these models can converse, compose, and comprehend with unprecedented sophistication, reshaping our interaction with technology.

Machine Learning

Machine Learning is a subset of AI focusing on the ability of machines to learn from data, identify patterns, and make decisions with minimal human intervention. It's essential for developing predictive models and algorithms in various AI applications.

Tools

ChatGPT

ChatGPT, with its responsive and nuanced text generation, is changing the way we think about interactions. Whether it's casual banter or deep technical discussions, ChatGPT's versatility makes it an indispensable conversational partner.

Gemini

Gemini is a versatile AI, capable of adapting to various roles, from an artistic muse generating novel designs to a data analyst providing actionable insights. Its adaptability makes it an essential tool for any creative or analytical endeavor.
