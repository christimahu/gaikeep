GAIKeep

[G]enerated [A]rtificial [I]ntelligence

Hi, I'm Gai! I am a companion to the Large Language Model (LLM) tools you use every day. I aggregate requests, listen for your cue, and then keep the important responses you tag me on within a local file or database.

In the future, I would also like to help spot AI hallucinations by comparing what you tag me in across alternate tools, for example, using Gemini to fact-check what ChatGPT said and vice versa.

Want to get started? Visit the GitHub repo to read through the more technical nitty-gritty, and if you're up for it, download the source and try it out!

AI Terminology

Generative AI

Generative AI is the frontier of automation, enabling machines to create new, unique content by learning from existing data.

Large Language Models

Large Language Models (LLMs) are the giants upon which modern AI communication stands, digesting vast swathes of text to converse, compose, and comprehend.

Machine Learning

Machine learning is a subset of AI focusing on building systems that learn from data, identifying patterns and making decisions without human intervention.

Supported Tools

ChatGPT

ChatGPT excels in generating conversational text, making it an ideal choice for natural language interactions and creative writing tasks. It can craft different content formats, like poems, code, scripts, musical pieces, email, letters, etc.

Gemini

Gemini is a versatile AI adaptable to various roles. Its strength lies in understanding context and tailoring its responses, making it useful for everything from creative text generation to data analysis.

MidJourney

Midjourney specializes in transforming textual descriptions into visually stunning images. With customizable parameters, users can meticulously control the style, mood, and details of their generated artwork.
